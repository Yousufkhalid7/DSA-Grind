172. Factorial Trailing Zeroes
Approach: When calculating factorials, multiples of 5 always have trailing 0.
N<5 -> No zeroes
5<N<10 -> 1 zero .....

Solution:
class Solution(object):
    def trailingZeroes(self, n):
        zeroes = 0
        while n:
            n//=5
            zeroes += n
        return zeroes
